About Hamsters

Hamsters are small, adorable rodents that make wonderful pets! They have soft fur, tiny paws, and cute cheeks that they fill with food.

Meet Our Hamster

🌟 小布丁 (Xiao Buding) is our beloved hamster!

Photo: A adorable hamster like 小布丁

Fun Facts About Hamsters

  • 🐹 Hamsters have cheek pouches where they store food!
  • 🌙 They are nocturnal - most active at night!
  • 🏃 They love to run on exercise wheels!
  • 💤 They can sleep up to 14-18 hours a day!
  • 🥗 Their favorite foods include seeds, vegetables, and fruits!

Hamster Care Tips

Taking care of 小布丁 requires love, attention, and proper care:

  • 🏠 Provide a spacious cage with bedding
  • 🥒 Fresh water and balanced diet daily
  • 🧹 Clean the cage regularly
  • 🎾 Give toys and exercise opportunities
  • ❤️ Handle gently and build trust

Hamster Personality

小布丁 has a wonderful personality! Hamsters are known for being:

  • Curious and adventurous
  • Sometimes shy at first, then very friendly
  • Love to explore and discover new things
  • Can be very affectionate with their owners
